India will celebrate Maha Shivratri this year on 21 February 2020. The 14th day of every lunar month, the day before the Amavasya or new moon is referred to as Shivratri. Of the twelve Shivratris that occur in a calendar year, the one that occurs in the lunar month of Magha is called Mahashivratri because it is the most powerful of the twelve Shivratris.

Kamali

Kamali is a Kannada drama television series starring Amulya, Rachana and Niranjan. Kamali hails from a poor family and dreams to excel in studies, but the bitter past of her family holds her back. Kamali, a village girl and a Kabbadi enthusiast, has to pass through a lot of hurdles in order to fulfil her dreams.

Naagini

Naagini is a 2016 Indian Kannada-language supernatural fantasy television series that airs on Zee Kannada. The series, a remake of the Hindi serial Naaginn, is directed by Hayavadana. The series premiered on 8 February 2016 and airs Monday to Friday at 9:00 pm. The Hindi-dubbed version of the show airs on Zee Anmol since October 2016. The show also aired dubbed in Tamil as Naga Rani on Zee Tamizh, in Hindi as Naag Kanya on Zee Anmol and in Bhojpuri as Naag Kanya on Big Ganga.

Paaru

Paaru is a Kannada drama television series starring Vinaya Prasad and others. The story revolves around Akhilandeshwari, an arrogant and rich woman, who has the final say in everything. A poor girl falls in love with a rich boy while trying to overcome the social and class-based boundaries that have been imposed on her.

Aathma Bandhana

Aathma Bandhana is a 1992 Indian Kannada horror drama film directed by Srikanth Nahata and produced by M. Rajagopal. The film features Shashikumar, Jayapradha and Vaishnavi in the lead roles. The film is a revenge drama of a woman, played by Vaishnavi, who is killed by her in-laws mercilessly and her spirit entering into a doll.
